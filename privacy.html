Overview

SentinelAI ("we," "our," or "us") provides a Chrome browser extension designed to help you avoid accidentally sharing sensitive personal information with AI tools and web forms. This Privacy Policy explains what data the extension processes and how that data is handled.

The consumer version of SentinelAI is built privacy-first: scanning happens locally in your browser, and no account is required.

Data collection

We do not collect, sell, or rent personally identifiable information from individual users of the consumer extension. Sensitive text you type or paste is analyzed in real time and is not transmitted to SentinelAI servers.

The extension does not:

  • Record or store the full content of your prompts on our servers
  • Track browsing history across websites
  • Collect data for advertising purposes

Local processing

When you interact with supported AI platforms or forms, SentinelAI scans text locally using pattern-based detection running entirely within the extension. If sensitive information is detected, you receive an on-screen privacy warning before content is submitted.

Text-based files you paste or attach may also be read locally on your device for the same detection purposes. File contents are not uploaded to SentinelAI.

What is stored

The extension may store the following data locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage APIs:

  • Settings — such as whether privacy warnings are enabled
  • Privacy events (optional) — a local log of detections and your decisions (e.g., review, send, cancel), kept on-device only and limited to recent entries

This local data is not synced to SentinelAI unless you use an enterprise deployment explicitly configured by your organization.

Third-party services

The consumer extension does not send user content to third-party servers. The extension operates on websites you visit (such as AI chat platforms) but does not share your input with external analytics, advertising, or data brokers.

AI platforms themselves (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) have their own privacy policies governing data you choose to submit. SentinelAI helps you review content before submission but cannot control how those services handle data once sent.

Enterprise mode

Organizations using SentinelAI Enterprise may configure the extension to send scan metadata or events to their own internal compliance systems. In such deployments, data handling is governed by the customer's policies and agreements with their users — not by this consumer privacy policy.

Your rights

Because the consumer extension stores data locally on your device, you can clear extension data at any time by removing the extension or clearing its storage in Chrome settings. You may disable privacy warnings in the extension settings without uninstalling.
