Privacy Policy

SentinelAI is designed to detect sensitive data locally in the browser.

Data Collection

We do not collect or store personally identifiable information from users. Sensitive text is analyzed in real time and not retained.

Third-party services

The extension does not send user content to third-party servers unless explicitly configured in enterprise deployments.

Enterprise mode

Enterprise customers may configure SentinelAI to send scan events to their internal compliance systems. All processing is governed by customer policy.
