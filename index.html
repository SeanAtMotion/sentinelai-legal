SentinelAI

Privacy-first AI data protection extension

What we do

SentinelAI detects sensitive information (like emails, API keys, and identifiers) before it is submitted into AI tools, chat systems, or web forms.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell user data
  • We do not store raw sensitive text
  • We do not track browsing history

Data handling

Detection happens locally in the browser whenever possible. In enterprise mode, organizations may route scans through their own secured backend.
