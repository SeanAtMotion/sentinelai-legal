Privacy-first Chrome extension

Your privacy safety net for AI tools

SentinelAI detects sensitive information before you send it to ChatGPT, Claude, and other AI platforms — so you can catch mistakes before they become leaks.

Install on Chrome Read Privacy Policy

What we do

Real-time protection that runs locally in your browser while you work.

🔍

Detect sensitive data

Scans for SSNs, emails, phone numbers, API keys, credit cards, and more as you type or paste.

⚠️

Warn before you send

Shows a clear privacy alert with options to review your content, go back, or send anyway.

📎

Scan attached files

Text-based files pasted or uploaded into AI chats are checked with the same detection rules.

What we do not do

Built for trust. No hidden data collection.

  • ✕ We do not sell user data
  • ✕ We do not store raw sensitive text on our servers
  • ✕ We do not track your browsing history
  • ✓ Detection runs locally in your browser — you stay in control

Data handling

Detection happens entirely on your device. When you submit a prompt, SentinelAI analyzes the text locally and shows a warning if personal or sensitive information is found. Optional privacy events (what was detected and your decision) may be stored locally on your device only.

The consumer extension does not require an account or backend connection. Enterprise deployments may optionally route metadata to an organization's own secured infrastructure — governed entirely by customer policy.
